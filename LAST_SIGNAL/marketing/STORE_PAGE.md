## Long description

Humanity's cities are ash and its people are twisted into the **Corrupted**. One relay still broadcasts: the **Last Signal** station. You are its final operator — the **Signal Keeper** — and you will not let the transmission die.

**LAST SIGNAL** is a fast, moody arcade survivor (bullet-heaven roguelite). Your weapons fire on their own; your job is to *move*, read the swarm, and choose how your power grows. Every level-up forks your run. Every run makes the next one stronger.

- ⚡ **Auto-combat, pure movement.** Dodge oceans of enemies while your arsenal does the killing.
- 🔧 **Build a war machine.** 8 weapons × 8 levels + 17 passives = 25 upgrades and thousands of loadouts. Plasma Rifle, Shock Wave, Drone Companion, Energy Mine, Laser Beam, and more.
- 👹 **Face the horde and its masters.** Corrupted Walkers, Shadow Crawlers, Mutated Brutes, volatile Ruptors — and three telegraphed bosses ending with the dreaded **Signal Devourer**.
- 🌆 **Three sectors, one descent.** The Abandoned City, the Destroyed Research Facility, and the Corrupted Communication Tower — each deadlier than the last.
- 🛰 **Permanent progress.** Bank Scrap, upgrade the Station, unlock Endless mode and the Scavenger. Death is never wasted.
- ♿ **Respectful design.** Colorblind-safe pickups, screen-shake/flash toggles, one-hand touch controls, difficulty options, instant restart, 60 FPS on low-end hardware.

Restore the signal. Save what's left.
